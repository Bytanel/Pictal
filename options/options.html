Caption

  • Ctrl + C
  • + / - Zoom in / out

  • In album

  • Space, Right, PageDown, Wheel Down Step forward
  • Shift + Space, Left, PageUp, Wheel Up Step backward
  • Shift + Left / Right, Shift + PageUp / PageDown Step 5 items backward/forward
  • Home, End Jump to start/end
  • Shift + End Jump to number, or search for captions

  • On media (audio/video)

  • Space (+ Ctrl in album) Play/Pause
  • Shift + Space Show/Hide controls
  • Left / Right / Wheel (+ Ctrl in album) Backward/forward 5s (+Shift: 15s)
  • Up / Down Adjust volume
  • , / . Frame-step for video (assumes 25fps)
  • M Mute/unmute

  • Full-Zoom mode

  • Wheel to Zoom-in/Zoom-out:
    • Over the image/media
    • When cursor changes to zoom-in
  • Wheel to scroll album:
    • Outside of the image/media
    • Near (10%) the borders of the browser window
